About our Programme

At M-EIS, the PYP ignites curiosity in young learners (ages 3–12), helping them grow into confident thinkers who ask big questions, explore meaningful ideas, and learn with purpose and joy.

Through the MYP, students (ages 12–16) discover how learning connects to the real world, building their ability to think critically, act responsibly, and develop a strong voice in a global society.

The DP at M-EIS challenges students (ages 16–19) to reach their full academic potential while growing as open-minded, reflective individuals ready to lead, research, and thrive in a complex world.

Our Official Sponsers

At M-EIS, we are proudly connected to the International Baccalaureate, sharing its vision of developing lifelong learners who think critically, act responsibly, and engage globally in every aspect of education.

M-EIS operates under the guidance of Egypt’s Ministry of Education, ensuring we meet national educational standards while delivering a rich, well-rounded academic experience for all our students and families.

We collaborate with Oasis École Internationale through shared values ofinternational-mindedness, aligning with global teaching practices and offering students a strong, multicultural foundation for future success.
